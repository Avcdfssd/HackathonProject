About Us

Our Mission

At RareConnect, we aim to bridge the gap in healthcare for patients suffering from rare diseases in Nepal. Our mission is to create an ecosystem that connects patients, doctors, researchers, and non-profits to tackle rare diseases collaboratively and effectively.

What We Do

RareConnect is a platform designed to:

  • Spread awareness about rare diseases and their symptoms.
  • Empower patients to report symptoms and receive AI-driven diagnoses.
  • Enable doctors to diagnose and escalate unique cases to researchers.
  • Provide a streamlined way for patients to request financial support from non-profits.
  • Promote collaboration between healthcare professionals and researchers for proactive solutions.

Why RareConnect?

In a country like Nepal, where healthcare infrastructure is fragmented, RareConnect offers: faster diagnosis, financial accessibility, and proactive research opportunities. We believe no disease is too rare to diagnose, no treatment is too expensive to fund, and no life is too insignificant to save.
